AI-powered facilitation for Big Picture Event Storming sessions as a Miro plugin.

What is Coach Stormer?

Coach Stormer is a Miro plugin that brings AI-powered personas into your Big Picture Event Storming workshops. It acts as a virtual facilitator, guiding teams through collaborative modeling sessions with intelligent, adaptive behavior.

  • AI personas with distinct behavioral styles (collaborative, dominant, passive, skeptical)
  • Graph-based storyline built from Miro sticky notes and connectors
  • Automated phase management for Event Storming sessions
  • Human-in-the-loop interaction with AI tool calls
  • Multiple business domain support (VeloCity, Crowd Sorcery)
  • Scoring rules for evaluating Event Storming quality
