About

What is Coach Stormer?

Coach Stormer is a tool for learning facilitation of collaborative modeling techniques. It simulates the most realistic experience of facilitation sessions, including human interactions, group dynamics, and mood.

Virtual participants with unique personalities (collaborative, challenging, skeptical) are present during your facilitation session, reacting and contributing just like real workshop attendees. You practice guiding the session, handling different perspectives, and keeping the group on track.

Works with your existing Miro boards with no setup overhead. You stay in control and review every AI suggestion before it's applied.

Currently supporting Big Picture Event Storming. More modeling techniques coming soon.
